A model about the habits of people consuming foodstuffs and using utensils during meals.

The directory contains:

	2 BLNs
	
		meals_any_for
			difficult to handle with most algorithms because of the hard constraint for usesAnyIn
		meals_any_for_functional
			more efficient as it functionally determines usesAnyIn from usesAnyForIn
	
	1 MLN
	
		meals_any_for
			direct translation of the above BLN model
